in english 
Bilbao was founded in 1300 by Diego Lopez V de Haro. Bilbao is an industrial port city in northern Spain, is surrounded by green mountains
 It is the de facto capital of the Basque Country, with a center full of skyscrapers. It is famous for the Museum
Guggenheim Bilbao, designed by Frank Gehry, which sparked revitalization when it opened
in 1997. The museum houses notable modern and contemporary works, but it is the curvilinear building
 titanium clad which receives the most attention and Bilbao is famous for its The emblematic Museum
Guggenheim, famous for its architecture and collection of contemporary art. The city is also
 known for its delicious Basque cuisine, with a variety of traditional dishes such as pintxos and
cod pil-pil.
